I · About

Good products aren't built on ideas; they're built on what actually works.

: A working principle

I'm a product manager with 4+ years across fintech, SaaS, and enterprise workforce management, always at the intersection of customer insight and product delivery. I own roadmaps, run experiments, and keep cross-functional teams aligned without losing sight of what ships.

I make decisions from data before I make them from instinct. I build real-time dashboards and telemetry infrastructure early, so the team has signal, not noise. Ambiguous problems get broken into phased milestones; features ship weekly rather than waiting for the perfect release.

Most recently at Voicemaker, I led end-to-end delivery for 12+ concurrent features on a 500K+ user platform. Before that, workforce product at T.J. McCartney and fintech onboarding modernization at Ernst & Young in Chennai. I hold an MS in Engineering Management from Northeastern.
